LeadQual AI – AI Lead Qualification Agent for Dubai Real Estate
Never miss a lead. Qualify every website visitor automatically, 24/7.
LeadQual AI helps real estate agencies in Dubai and the Middle East filter and qualify website visitors before a human agent spends time calling them. The AI holds a natural conversation, scores the lead, and delivers a structured summary ready for follow-up.

The problem
Real estate agents often waste time calling leads who are not ready, have no clear budget, or are just browsing. LeadQual AI solves this by asking the key qualification questions automatically — before the human agent gets involved.

Who this is for
Real estate agencies in Dubai and the Middle East
Brokers who receive website inquiries
Teams that want faster handoff from website chat to human agent
Agencies planning to connect AI qualification with WhatsApp, CRM, or Google Sheets
Key benefits for your agency
Reduce time wasted on unqualified leads
Focus agents on high-potential prospects only
Respond instantly to every website visitor, 24/7
Receive clean, structured lead data ready for follow-up
Create a professional first impression every time
How it works
The AI engages every visitor in a friendly, natural conversation and collects:

Buy or rent intention
Budget
Preferred area in Dubai
Property type (apartment, villa, townhouse, etc.)
Timeline — when they want to move
Cash buyer or mortgage needed
Interest in a property viewing
Name and contact details
At the end, it generates a structured lead summary with a Hot / Warm / Cold score and a suggested next action for the agent.

Lead scoring logic
Hot
Clear budget & area
Short timeline
Strong intent
Viewing interest
Cash or mortgage plan confirmed
Warm
Some clear preferences
Budget or area known
Medium-term timeline
Interested but not urgent
Cold
Just browsing
Unclear budget
No defined area
Not ready to move soon
